# Project Impact

This folder explains why the forecasting work mattered from a supply-chain planning perspective.

The original impact notebooks were removed because they contained embedded outputs, internal paths, and row-level supplier/facility/part identifiers. Their useful ideas were consolidated into `project_impact_summary.md`.

Key impact themes:

- Forecasting demand at the supplier-part level helps planning teams prepare for shipment risk earlier.
- Modeling many sparse series jointly with TFT reduces reliance on one-off per-series models.
- Leakage-safe validation gives a more realistic picture of forward-looking performance.
- Supplier, part, and global demand features connect model behavior to operational drivers.
- Aggregate metrics such as WAPE communicate forecast quality in a way that reflects business volume.

See `project_impact_summary.md` for the polished public narrative.
